# Cash Flow Dashboard & Liquidity Monitoring

## Overview
This repository contains practical tools and dashboards used to
monitor, forecast, and manage organisational liquidity.

The purpose is to provide real-time visibility into cash position,
upcoming obligations, and expected inflows, enabling proactive and
informed financial decision-making.

---

## Objectives
• Maintain clear visibility over available cash  
• Anticipate short-term funding pressures  
• Support payment prioritisation  
• Improve forecasting accuracy  
• Provide management and board-ready insights  

---

## Dashboard Categories

### Current Cash Position
Bank balances, overdraft utilisation, and available liquidity.

### Short-Term Forecasting
Expected receipts and payments over the coming weeks.

### Cash Runway
Projection of how long existing cash resources will sustain operations.

### Working Capital Drivers
Debtor collections, creditor payments, and timing assumptions.

### Variance Analysis
Actual vs forecast performance and trend monitoring.

---

## Typical Outputs
• Weekly liquidity view  
• Funding gap identification  
• Payment risk visibility  
• Covenant and facility monitoring  
• Executive summary indicators  

---

## How This Is Used
These dashboards are commonly used for:
• Weekly finance reviews  
• Management meetings  
• Board reporting  
• Funding negotiations  
• Operational planning  

---

## Design Principles
• Clear separation between assumptions and calculations  
• Simple, transparent formulas  
• Visual management summaries  
• Flexible for different business sizes  
• Audit and review friendly
